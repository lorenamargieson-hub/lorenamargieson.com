About

A graphic designer of 15 years, working across brand, editorial, product and web from Valla, NSW.

Currently Co-Founder and Head of Product and Design at Booking My Surf, where I shape the brand, the product and every touchpoint in between. Alongside that, I maintain a small freelance practice — a considered, selective approach to clients and fully remote collaboration.

My work is grounded in clear communication, strong systems and long-term thinking. I have developed a focus on educational and community-based projects, particularly within First Nations contexts, where cultural respect and the protection of knowledge are essential.

With a background in sustainable operations, I approach design as part of a broader system — considering environmental impact, longevity and responsible production at every stage.
